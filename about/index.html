  • Nice to meet you, I’m Kyle Meyer, a designer based in San Francisco.

    Over the last 18 years, I have designed software used by billions of people, explored new paths for companies, and built and directed design teams. I’ve worked with and learned much from many great people along the way.

    Recently, I departed Slack after blazing a path for productivity features with Slack Canvas and Slack Lists. Projects set in motion based on exploratory work at Quip—where I worked with Salesforce and Quip executive leadership to envision the future of productivity and communication software—prior to Salesforce’s acquisition of Slack.

    Previously, I led design on a variety of projects at Facebook including Timeline, Profiles, and Groups; as well as establishing a consistent design system for Entities such as Pages, Events, Profiles, and more. Before that, I helped bring art to the cart at Big Cartel by improving store customization and order management for small e-commerce businesses.

    I have also helped clients including Quora, Blue Bottle Coffee, Quip, Salesforce, Build Conference, Worrell, The Manual, and AIGA.
